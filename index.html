To put this on his iPad permanently:

  2. Create a new repo called samrath-world
  3. Upload this HTML file as index.html
  4. Go to Settings > Pages > Branch: main
  5. Your URL will be: username.github.io/samrath-world
  6. Bookmark that URL in Safari on his iPad
  7. Tap "Add to Home Screen" — it looks like an app
